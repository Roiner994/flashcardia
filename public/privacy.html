Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Flashcardia ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Information: When you sign up or log in (using email/password or Google Sign-In), we collect your name, email address, and profile picture. This is facilitated securely through Supabase Auth and Google OAuth.
  • User Content: We store the flashcards, categories, learning history, and progress you create within the app to sync them across your devices.
  • Audio Permissions: The app requests permission to record audio to support features like speech recognition. Voice recordings are processed locally or via Google's speech recognition APIs and are not stored permanently on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • Sync your flashcard decks, study sessions, and progress data.
  • Maintain, personalize, and improve the features of the App.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information with third-party service providers essential to the app's functionality:

  • Supabase: For backend database services, hosting, and user authentication.
  • Google Sign-In: To authenticate your Google account.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the settings page inside the app.
  • Alternatively, you can request complete account and data deletion at any time by emailing us at roiner.hernandez.dev@gmail.com. Upon request, we will delete all your personal data and flashcard information within 7 business days.

5. Security of Your Data

The security of your personal data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption and security services provided by Supabase to protect your authentication and database credentials.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
